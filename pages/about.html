About Me

My Story

Hi! I'm Meshack Tirop, a Kenyan-born developer currently pursuing Computer Engineering in South Korea. My journey into technology started with curiosity about computers during my early years in Kenya, which evolved into a career path spanning web development, AI, and cybersecurity.

Studying in South Korea has broadened my perspective and allowed me to collaborate on global projects, pushing the boundaries of what's possible with technology. I'm passionate about creating solutions that make a real impact in people's lives.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing my knowledge with fellow developers in the tech community.

What Drives Me

I believe technology should be accessible, inclusive, and meaningful. Every line of code I write is guided by the principle of creating solutions that not only work but also improve user experiences and solve real-world problems.

My multicultural experience, having lived in both Kenya and South Korea, gives me a unique perspective on global technology needs and user experiences across different cultures and contexts.

I'm constantly learning and adapting to new technologies, always seeking to expand my skillset and stay at the forefront of technological innovation.
